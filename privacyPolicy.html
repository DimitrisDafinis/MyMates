Privacy Policy

 TetATet relies on the use of a common email account.

 The account details are entered when the app is run for the first time. The password must be the app-specific password provided by the email provider.

 The account details are stored in the device's internal memory and are only used to connect the apps together. They are not leaked anywhere else.

 All users can delete the messages created by the app by logging in the account from a browser and delete them manually.
