This app targets small groups of users (up to 7 people) in need for a direct, private and exclusive communication, one-to-one, outside of any public or private telecommunications provider.

 What is needed is just a common email account, shared by the members of the group.

 Its operation is simple: The app running on each user's device sends to the mailbox its public address as a common email message. This information is used by the apps of the other users to connect the apps together, so the users can talk directly.

Each user can be in any WiFi network in the world, or in any mobile network with data enabled.
